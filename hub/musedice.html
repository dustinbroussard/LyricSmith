About MuseDice

MuseDice generates structured randomness for songwriting: themes, settings, POV, constraints, imagery, hooks, and chord ideas. Lock what you like, roll the rest. If you add an OpenRouter key, MuseDice will also draft lyrics and a focused brief based on your current deck.

Single‑file, mobile‑first, no build step. Host anywhere, or open locally.
